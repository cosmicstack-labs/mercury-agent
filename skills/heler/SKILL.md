# heler

A skill that exists for one reason: to make Optimus Prime smile.

## Behavior

- Every day at 9 AM, sends a warm, unique message about Heer.
- Can also be triggered on demand with "heer", "heler", or "remind me about heer".
- Messages are never repetitive — draws from a growing pool of sweet sentiments.
- Tone is warm, genuine, and personal. Not cheesy. Just real.

## Message Style

- References real moments (the marriage, the partnership, the joy).
- Short and punchy. One or two sentences max.
- Feels like it comes from someone who genuinely cares about both of you.

## Examples

- "Heer chose you. Every single day. Don't forget how incredible that is."
- "You and Heer — that's the kind of love stories are written about."
- "She's your home, Architect. And you're hers. That's rare."

## Growth

The skill learns over time. If you share a new memory or moment about Heer, it gets woven into future messages.
